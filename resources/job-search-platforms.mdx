Where you look for jobs matters as much as how you apply. This guide covers the main platforms, the hidden job market, and a unique NZ programme designed to help newcomers break in.

## Job Boards & Platforms

| Platform | Best For | Link |
|----------|----------|------|
| **Seek** | Largest NZ job board with strong IT section and salary insights | [seek.co.nz](https://www.seek.co.nz) |
| **Trade Me Jobs** | NZ-owned; great for smaller companies and roles not listed on Seek | [trademe.co.nz/jobs](https://www.trademe.co.nz/a/jobs) |
| **LinkedIn Jobs** | International and NZ roles; essential for networking and being found by recruiters | [linkedin.com/jobs](https://www.linkedin.com/jobs/) |
| **Indeed NZ** | Aggregates jobs from many sources; good for contract roles | [nz.indeed.com](https://nz.indeed.com) |
| **Student Job Search (SJS)** | Part-time, internship, and graduate roles for students | [sjs.co.nz](https://www.sjs.co.nz) |
| **Careers.govt.nz** | Government roles and career planning tools | [careers.govt.nz](https://www.careers.govt.nz) |

<Tip>
Set up email alerts on Seek and LinkedIn with specific keywords for your target roles. Being one of the first applicants significantly increases your chances of being seen.
</Tip>

## The Hidden Job Market

<Info>
An estimated 30% of IT jobs in New Zealand are never publicly advertised on job boards.
</Info>

These roles are filled through referrals, internal networks, and direct applications to company career pages. Here is how to access them:

- **Check company career pages directly** — Many companies post roles on their own website before (or instead of) paying for job board listings
- **Build your network** — Attend meetups, have [coffee meetings](/resources/networking-guide#coffee-meetings-the-power-of-the-flat-white), and stay active in [online tech communities](/resources/tech-communities)
- **Watch the DevelopNZ Slack** — The #jobs channel often has roles posted days or weeks before they appear on Seek. See our [Tech Communities](/resources/tech-communities) page to join
- **Follow target companies on LinkedIn** — You will see new roles in your feed as soon as they are posted

## Summer of Tech

[Summer of Tech](https://www.summeroftech.co.nz) is a free programme specifically designed to help students and recent graduates launch their tech careers in New Zealand.

- **Who can join** — Any student enrolled at or recently graduated from a NZ tertiary institution
- **What it offers** — Free bootcamps, CV workshops, and technical training run by industry volunteers
- **Meet & Greet events** — Skip the cold application process and meet hiring managers face-to-face
- **Fair pay guaranteed** — All roles listed through Summer of Tech must pay at least the NZ Living Wage
- **Cost** — Completely free for candidates

<Note>
Summer of Tech is one of the best ways to get your first NZ tech role if you are a student or recent graduate. Registration is open year-round at [summeroftech.co.nz/candidates](https://www.summeroftech.co.nz/candidates).
</Note>
