Recruitment agencies can connect you with roles you will not find on job boards — and they are completely free for job seekers. The employer pays the agency fee, never you.

## How Recruitment Agencies Work

- **Free for you** — You never pay a recruitment agency to find work. If anyone asks you to pay, that is a red flag
- **Register with 2–3 agencies** — This maximises your coverage without spreading yourself too thin
- **Be honest** — Share your visa status, notice period, salary expectations, and career goals upfront. The more they know, the better they can match you
- **They are your advocate** — Think of recruiters as your marketing team. Give them clear examples of your strengths so they can pitch you effectively to employers
- **Stay in touch** — Check in regularly and update them when your availability or preferences change

<Tip>
When registering, have a clean, up-to-date CV ready. Many agencies (like Beyond Recruitment) will ask about your visa status and notice period during registration — have these details prepared.
</Tip>

## AI Tools for Job Applications

These AI tools can help you optimise your CV, practise for interviews, and improve your communication skills. Use them to prepare and build confidence.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Jobscan" icon="magnifying-glass-chart" href="https://www.jobscan.co">
    Compares your CV against a job description and shows how well they match. Identifies missing keywords and formatting issues that cause automated screening systems (ATS) to reject applications.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Google Interview Warmup" icon="google" href="https://grow.google/certificates/interview-warmup/">
    Free tool from Google. Practise answering common interview questions with voice — it analyses your responses and highlights areas to improve.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Yoodli" icon="comments" href="https://www.yoodli.ai">
    AI speech coach that analyses your speaking pace, filler words (um, ah), and clarity. Especially helpful if English is not your first language.
  </Card>
  <Card title="OfferGenie" icon="wand-magic-sparkles" href="https://offergenie.ai">
    All-in-one tool combining interview coaching, STAR-method feedback, CV optimisation, and job application tracking.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Thita.ai" icon="robot" href="https://thita.ai">
    Comprehensive interview preparation platform covering behavioural, technical, and system design questions with AI-powered feedback.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Huru.ai" icon="graduation-cap" href="https://huru.ai">
    AI mock interviews with in-depth analysis. Good for practising data science, architecture, and complex technical scenarios.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
Use AI tools to **prepare and practise**, not to misrepresent your skills. Tools that provide real-time answers during live interviews exist, but using them is dishonest and risky — the NZ tech community is small, and your reputation matters more than any single job.
</Note>
