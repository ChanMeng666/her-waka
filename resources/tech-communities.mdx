## LinkedIn Groups

LinkedIn has many active tech groups where you can learn from others, share ideas, and discover job opportunities. To find relevant groups:

1. Go to [linkedin.com/groups](https://www.linkedin.com/groups)
2. Search for topics that interest you (e.g., "women in tech", "data analytics", "web development")
3. Join groups that are active and welcoming to beginners
4. Engage with posts and introduce yourself
