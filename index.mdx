# HER WAKA

**Helping women navigate their career journey with confidence.**

HER WAKA is an employment-focused programme delivered by SheSharp in partnership with the Ministry of Social Development. Running from March to June 2026, the programme supports women in Auckland through interactive workshops covering AI skills, job readiness, employment rights, and professional networking.
