# About HER WAKA

HER WAKA is an employment-focused programme designed to empower women in Auckland who are seeking employment or looking to re-enter the workforce. Delivered by SheSharp in partnership with the Ministry of Social Development (MSD), the programme runs from March to June 2026.

## Mission

To equip participants with practical skills, knowledge, and confidence to navigate the modern job market — from understanding their employment rights to leveraging AI tools in their career journey.

## Who Is It For?

HER WAKA is designed for MSD clients in the Auckland region — women who are actively seeking employment or preparing to return to the workforce. No prior tech experience is required.

## Programme Components

The programme is built around five key components delivered across four interactive workshop sessions:

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Speakers** | Industry professionals share career insights and real-world experiences |
| **RCSA Insights** | Guidance from the Recruitment, Consulting and Staffing Association on working with recruiters |
| **Job Readiness** | CV writing, interview preparation, LinkedIn optimisation, and cover letter skills |
| **Employment Rights** | Understanding your rights as an employee in Aotearoa New Zealand |
| **Networking** | Building professional connections and growing your career network |

## Expected Outcomes

By completing HER WAKA, participants will:

- Understand how to use AI tools to support their job search
- Have an updated CV and LinkedIn profile
- Know their employment rights under New Zealand law
- Feel confident networking with professionals and recruiters
- Be connected to the SheSharp community for ongoing support
