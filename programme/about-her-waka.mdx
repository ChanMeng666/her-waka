# About HER WAKA

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/banners/her-waka-banner-portrait.png" alt="HER WAKA — Your Journey. Your Pace. Your Path." />
</Frame>

**Navigating pathways into sustainable employment.**

HER WAKA is a dynamic, employment-focused programme delivered by SheSharp in Auckland for Ministry of Social Development (MSD) clients who are ready to strengthen their pathway into work. Running monthly from March to June 2026, each session is designed to build job readiness, confidence, and practical understanding of the New Zealand labour market.

## Mission

To equip participants with practical skills, knowledge, and confidence to navigate the modern job market — from understanding their employment rights to leveraging AI tools in their career journey.

## Who Is It For?

HER WAKA is designed for MSD clients in the Auckland region who are ready to take their next step into meaningful work. No prior tech experience is required.

## Programme Format

Each two-hour session brings together up to 25 participants in a supportive, high-impact environment that blends inspiration, practical skills, and direct employer insight. Through real conversations and structured learning, participants gain clarity on how to position themselves confidently and competitively in today's job market.

## What to Expect

- **Inspirational speakers** sharing authentic employment journeys and practical lessons from navigating the New Zealand workforce
- **Employer and recruiter panels** offering direct insight into current hiring expectations
- **Practical job-readiness sessions** focused on professional presentation, interview confidence, and effective employer engagement
- **Clear and accessible employment rights guidance** to help you understand your rights at work
- **Structured networking opportunities** with employers, recruiters, and industry representatives

## Programme Components

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Speakers** | Industry professionals share career insights and real-world experiences |
| **RCSA Insights** | Guidance from the Recruitment, Consulting and Staffing Association on the hiring landscape and working with recruiters |
| **Job Readiness** | Professional presentation, interview confidence, and effective employer engagement |
| **Employment Rights** | Understanding your rights as an employee in Aotearoa New Zealand |
| **Networking** | Structured opportunities to connect with employers, recruiters, and industry representatives |

## Expected Outcomes

By completing HER WAKA, participants will:

- Understand how to use AI tools to support their job search
- Have an updated CV and LinkedIn profile
- Know their employment rights under New Zealand law
- Feel confident networking with professionals and recruiters
- Be connected to the SheSharp community for ongoing support
