**SheSharp** is a not-for-profit organisation founded in 2014, dedicated to supporting women in technology and digital careers across Aotearoa New Zealand.

## Our Mission

SheSharp exists to inspire, educate, and connect women in tech. We believe that diversity drives innovation, and we work to remove barriers that prevent women from entering and thriving in the technology sector.

## What We Do

SheSharp delivers programmes, workshops, and community events that support women at every stage of their career journey — from those taking their first steps into tech to experienced professionals looking to grow.

### Past Initiatives

- **Community workshops** on web development, data skills, and digital literacy
- **Mentorship programmes** connecting emerging talent with industry professionals
- **Networking events** bringing together women in tech across Auckland
- **Partnerships** with government agencies and industry bodies to deliver employment-focused programmes

## Delivering HER WAKA

SheSharp was selected by the Ministry of Social Development to deliver the HER WAKA programme based on our track record of running accessible, high-quality digital skills programmes for diverse communities.
