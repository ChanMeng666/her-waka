# AI & Future of Work

**Session 1 — March 25, 2026**

The first HER WAKA workshop explores how artificial intelligence is changing the workplace and how participants can use AI tools to support their employment journey.

## Instructor

**Chan Meng** — Technology educator and SheSharp facilitator.

## Agenda

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| Welcome | Introductions and programme overview |
| Session 1 | What is AI? Understanding the basics |
| Session 2 | AI tools for job seekers — a guided tour |
| Break | Morning tea and networking |
| Session 3 | Hands-on exercises with AI tools |
| Wrap-up | Q&A, resources, and next steps |

## Prerequisites

- No prior tech experience required
- Bring a laptop if you have one (devices can be provided)
- Come ready to explore and ask questions

## What You Will Learn

- What AI is and how it is being used in workplaces today
- How to use AI tools like ChatGPT to write cover letters and prepare for interviews
- How to search for jobs more effectively using AI-powered platforms
- Ethical considerations when using AI in your career

<Note>
This workshop is hands-on. You will get to try the tools yourself with guided support. See the [hands-on exercises](/workshop/march-2026/hands-on-exercises) for details.
</Note>
