The fourth and final cohort of the HER WAKA series focuses on **personal branding and career growth** — treating your career like a Go-To-Market strategy.

**Andrea Halal**, Tech Marketing Leader at Ideqa and creator of *Big Leap Energy*, will share how she rebuilt her network and professional credibility after moving to New Zealand with no local connections. Drawing on nine years of marketing experience across TV, production, advertising, brand management, and B2B marketing, Andrea will walk participants through the practical steps for building visibility and trust when you are new, relocating, or pivoting.

Expect a grounded, strategy-driven session on using LinkedIn as a career tool (not a highlight reel), on why positioning matters as much as performance, and on how relationships quietly compound over time.

As with every HER WAKA cohort, **RCSA** will join the session to share insights on the current New Zealand job market and offer practical guidance on job-seeking.

## What to Expect

- A **personal branding masterclass** with Andrea Halal — career as a GTM strategy
- Practical steps for **building visibility, credibility, and networks** from scratch
- How to use **LinkedIn as a career tool** — positioning, presence, and quiet relationship-building
- **RCSA insights** — current job market updates and practical jobseeker guidance
