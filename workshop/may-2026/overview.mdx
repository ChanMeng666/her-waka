The third cohort of the HER WAKA series continues with a session focused on **cybersecurity**. Participants will hear from three industry experts through short lightning talks, each sharing a unique perspective and experience in the cybersecurity space. This is an opportunity to gain a broader understanding of the field, the different pathways into it, and the real-world challenges and opportunities it presents.

In the second half of the session, participants will take part in a **45-minute recruiter networking activity** with RCSA representatives. There will be 2–3 tables set up where small groups can connect directly with recruiters, ask questions, and practise real-world conversations in a supportive environment.

## What to Expect

- Three **cybersecurity lightning talks** from senior professionals — including business analysis, AI/cyber consultancy, and agile transformation perspectives
- A **45-minute RCSA recruiter networking activity** — small-group rotations with multiple recruiters
- A grounded view of cybersecurity career pathways and the in-demand skills shaping safer digital workplaces

## Lightning Talk Speakers

### Prasanth Pavithran

<img src="/images/speakers/prasanth-pavithran.jpg" alt="Prasanth Pavithran" width="300" style={{ borderRadius: '12px' }} />

Prasanth Pavithran works at the intersection of business architecture, transformation, people, and technology. With an MBA and over two decades of global experience, he serves as Senior Business Analyst in the Office of the CTO at Auckland University of Technology, mapping the relationships between strategy, capability, process, and technology. Before AUT he led a business analysis team at healthAlliance and spent nearly a decade at HCL Technologies delivering large-scale SAP programmes. He has been mentoring with SheSharp since 2017.

### Paula Gair

<img src="/images/speakers/paula-gair.jpg" alt="Paula Gair" width="300" style={{ borderRadius: '12px' }} />

Paula Gair advises, teaches and assesses Master's students at Tech Futures Lab on multiple aspects of emerging and disruptive technology — including AI, cybersecurity, privacy, and embedding Security and Privacy by Design. As founder of **deriskme**, she works with families, SMEs, schools and businesses on practical cyber security, online safety, and privacy.
