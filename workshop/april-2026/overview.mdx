**Session 2 — Tuesday 7 April 2026 | 12:00 pm – 2:00 pm**
**academyEX Pikopiko, Auckland**

The second session of the HER WAKA series focuses on building confidence, celebrating achievements, and exploring practical AI tools.

We begin with **#IAmRemarkable**, a global movement that encourages everyone — especially those from underrepresented groups — to celebrate their achievements and challenge the stigma around self-promotion. SheSharp Founder and Director **Dr Mahsa McCauley** will guide participants through practical exercises to help recognise achievements, own your story, and confidently share your impact.

We will also introduce **Vibe Coding**, showing how anyone — even without a technical background — can use free AI tools to build and launch their own personal website through simple, natural conversations with AI.

As with the first session, **RCSA** will join to share valuable insights on the current job market and support participants with practical job-seeking guidance.

## What to Expect

- A 50-minute **#IAmRemarkable workshop** — recognise your achievements and learn to share them with confidence
- A **hands-on Vibe Coding session** — build and launch your own personal website using free AI tools
- **RCSA insights** — current job market updates and practical job-seeking guidance
